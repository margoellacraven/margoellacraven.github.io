Hi, I'm Margo! Thanks for visiting my page!

I am a third year journalism and film student at Fordham University in New York City. This is where you can get to know me a little better! I am orginally from Bend, Oregon, and came to New York City to pursue writing and film. In my free time, I enjoy reading, playing guitar, and exploring the city with friends.
